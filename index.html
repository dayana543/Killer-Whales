Orcas

Orcas, or killer whales, are the largest of the dolphins and one of the world's most powerful predators.Orcas hunt in deadly pods,family groups of up to 40 individuals.These different groups may prey on different animals and use different techniques to catch them.Resident pods tend to prefer fish, while transient pods target marine mammals. All pods use effective, cooperative hunting techniques that some liken to the behavior of wolf packs.
